Frequently Asked Questions

What data does RosterIQ access?

RosterIQ reads employee info, schedules, timesheets, and leave from Tanda, plus signals from your POS (if available), weather APIs, event calendars, and booking platforms. We never store personal data—only aggregate forecasting signals.

How long does setup take?

Click "Install RosterIQ," log in to your Tanda account, and grant permissions. You'll generate your first roster draft within 5 minutes. Configuration and fine-tuning may take 30–60 minutes for optimal results.

Which venues and industries are supported?

RosterIQ is built for Australian hospitality (bars, restaurants, hotels, cafes). Multi-venue operators get the most value. We are expanding to other regions and industries—contact support for early access.

Can I change or cancel my subscription?

Yes. Switch between tiers anytime within your account settings. Cancel your subscription and RosterIQ will be disabled immediately. Your data in Tanda is never affected.

How is my data protected?

RosterIQ uses OAuth 2.0 (same standard Tanda uses). We never see your Tanda password. All data is encrypted in transit and encrypted at rest. We comply with Australian Privacy Principles.
